Sk. Tanvir Hossain

Currently I am an adjunct lecturer at the Economics Department of East West University, Bangladesh. From 2018 - 2022, as a doctoral student, I was a research associate (in German Wissenschaftlicher Mitarbeiter) at the Department of Statistics (Chair of Business and Social Statistics) at Technical University of Dortmund (TU Dortmund), Germany. I am still enrolled as a doctoral student at the Business and Economics Department of TU Dortmund and working on my dissertation.

I completed M.Sc. in Economics from the University of Mannheim, Germany, and Bachelors in Business with a second major in Economics from BRAC University, Bangladesh. My research interests lie at the intersection of Causal Inference, Econometrics, and Machine Learning (both theoretical and applied works), and I love coding.
